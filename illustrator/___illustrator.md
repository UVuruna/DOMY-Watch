# illustrator/

Adobe Illustrator vector sources for skin assets — NOT bundled with the app.
Final assets are exported from here into
[Assets (folder)](../assets/___assets.md) as SVG/PNG.

## Files

- `Clock 24h.ai`, `Clock.ai`, `Clock_small.ai` — dial variants
- `Clock_Circle.ai`, `Clock_OuterColors.ai` — ring and sector coloring
- `AnnoLucis.ai` — lettering/wordmark artwork
