# Chinese Zodiac — Bronze Plates v2 (Gemini)

The owner's verdict on the current bronze set: the animals hold up,
but the border is a **Greek meander on a Chinese plate** — a genuine
style clash once you see it. This sheet regenerates all twelve with:

1. a strictly CHINESE border — the key-fret (**huiwen**, the "thunder
   pattern": China's own squared spiral, so the visual rhythm of the
   old border survives, just authentically) plus ruyi cloud scrolls;
2. the **five Wu Xing element roundels ON the border ring**, at the
   five points of a pentagram exactly as the owner's reference
   diagram: FIRE 火 top, EARTH 土 upper right, METAL 金 lower right,
   WATER 水 lower left, WOOD 木 upper left (the generative cycle
   read clockwise);
3. bronze dominant, ONE bright accent per animal (the Rooster's red
   crest rule) beside the colored roundels.

**Metal-swap note (technical):** the app's gold/silver styles run a
hue-selective swap on WARM BRONZE pixels (hue window ~10–60°). Keep
the EARTH roundel deep RED-BROWN (not yellow-ochre) and the accents
red — both sit below the window, so the swap recolors only the
bronze, never the enamel. The prompts below already say so.

**Drop:** over the existing stems in `assets/zodiac/chinese/`
(`Rat.png` … `Pig.png` — capitalized). The colored set
(`chinese_colored/`) is untouched.

Shared skeleton (identical in every prompt): *Ornate circular
medallion, weathered bronze sculptural relief on dark cracked stone,
photorealistic render, perfectly centered, isolated on white
background… Border: dark bronze ring carved with the Chinese key-fret
(huiwen) thunder pattern and ruyi cloud scrolls — strictly Chinese
ornament, no Greek meander — with five small enamel element roundels
set into the ring at the five points of a pentagram: fire 火 at the
very top on deep red, earth 土 at the upper right on deep red-brown,
metal 金 at the lower right on pale gold, water 水 at the lower left
on deep blue, wood 木 at the upper left on green.*
