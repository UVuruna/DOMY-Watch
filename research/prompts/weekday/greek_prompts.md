# Greek Gods — the Complete Prompt Sheet (bronze + colored, Planetary + Pantheon)

This is the ONE sheet for the Greek weekday theme — every image the
theme needs, both rosters, both finishes, generated top to bottom.
It supersedes all earlier partial versions of `greek_prompts.md` and
folds in the colored badges that used to live only in
`colored_badge_prompts.md`.

**Border identity (bronze plates):** a bronze ring carved as a
continuous **Greek key (meander) band**, running clockwise, broken
every quarter turn by a **laurel-wreath roundel** that cradles the
day's planetary glyph in relief — distinct from every other bronze
theme's border (no knotwork, no hieroglyphs, no paw-prints).

**Border identity (colored plates):** the same Greek-key meander,
recut as a polished-metal ring (gold, darkened gold, rose gold or
silver depending on the figure) with small enamel roundels bearing
the seat's own emblem — never the bronze ring, never bare metal.

**Style note:** aged bronze relief is the base register for the
bronze plates because this is the metal-swap family — gold and
silver variants hue-swap the same warm bronze pixels
programmatically, so no separate gold/silver prompts are needed for
bronze. The colored plates are a fresh full-color badge each, not a
recolor of the bronze art.

**House rules (every plate, both rosters, both finishes):** circular
medallion or badge, photorealistic render, perfectly centered,
isolated on white background. Never write letters or words into the
image — no lettering, no text, no watermark, anywhere.

**Drop directories:**
- Planetary bronze → `assets/weekday/greek/primary/<file>.png` (dual
  → `primary/dual/<file>.png`)
- Planetary colored → `assets/weekday/greek/colored/<file>.png` (dual
  → `colored/dual/<file>.png`)
- Pantheon bronze → `assets/weekday/greek/pantheon/<file>.png` (dual
  → `pantheon/dual/<file>.png`; the ninth `gaia.png` drops straight in
  `pantheon/`, no subfolder)
- Pantheon colored → `assets/weekday/greek/pantheon/colored/<file>.png`
  (dual → `pantheon/colored/dual/<file>.png`)

**SUPERSEDED — read before generating anything:** earlier versions of
this sheet carried Hades as the Greek NINTH (`hades.png` /
`colored/hades.png`, owner's "8+1 extension," 2026-07-14). That
placement is retired. Under the crystallized doctrine
(`research/pantheon_catalog.md`, round two/four/five, 2026-07-15),
the Underworld lives at the DUAL's shadow face, not the ninth: the
Pantheon Greek dual is **Zeus / Hades** — the throne above and the
throne below — and the Pantheon ninth is **Gaia**, the Union of both
lines. **Do not generate a ninth Hades plate, bronze or colored.**
Hades's art is the Pantheon DUAL plate below instead
(`pantheon/dual/hades.png` and `pantheon/colored/dual/hades.png`).
