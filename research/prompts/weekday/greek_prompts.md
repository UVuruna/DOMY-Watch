# Greek Gods — the Olympian Weekday Theme (bronze plates)

The Olympian set — Helios, Selene, Ares, Hermes, Zeus, Aphrodite,
Cronus, plus the Sunday dual Phaethon — is one of the two oldest
weekday themes and predates the prompt-sheet convention entirely; this
sheet regenerates all eight plates from the canon in
`Database/symbolism.json` so the owner can drop fresh renders straight
over the existing files.

**Border identity:** a bronze ring carved as a continuous **Greek key
(meander) band**, running clockwise, broken every quarter turn by a
**laurel-wreath roundel** that cradles the day's planetary glyph in
relief — distinct from every other bronze theme's border (no
knotwork, no hieroglyphs, no paw-prints).

**Style note:** aged bronze relief is the base register because this
is the metal-swap family — gold and silver variants hue-swap the same
warm bronze pixels programmatically, so no separate gold/silver
prompts are needed here.
