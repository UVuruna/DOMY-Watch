# Planetary Sign Glyphs — Metal Sigil Plates (ChatGPT)

The owner is regenerating the `planet_signs` weekday theme from
scratch: the seven classical planetary glyphs plus the Sunday dual,
each as one polished-metal sigil floating on a starfield disc — no
creature, no scene, just the glyph itself dressed in its day's metal
and hue. Authored fresh from the project's own canon:

- **Metal per day** — the seven classical planet-metals already fixed
  in [Alchemy Metal Prompts](alchemy_metal_prompts.md) (Sun=Gold,
  Moon=Silver, Mars=Iron, Mercury=quicksilver, Jupiter=Tin,
  Venus=Copper, Saturn=Lead), reused here so the two metal-themed sets
  agree.
- **Hue per day** — the six dial-arm colors from
  `Database/symbolism.json` → `arms` (Monday blue, Tuesday orange,
  Wednesday purple, Thursday yellow, Friday red, Saturday green) plus
  the Sun's own white-gold, which sits at the dial's center rather
  than on an arm (per the Leo article: "the white light of Sunday from
  which all six arm colors are decomposed").

**Canon rule, kept:** this theme wears NO text labels on the dial —
every prompt below repeats "no text, no watermark, no labels"
accordingly.
