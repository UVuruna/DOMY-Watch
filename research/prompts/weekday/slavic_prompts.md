# Slavic Gods — the Rodnovery Weekday Theme (bronze plates + colored)

The Slavic set — Dažbog, Hors, Svetovid, Veles, Perun, Mokoš, Morana,
plus the Sunday dual Old Dažbog — predates the prompt-sheet convention
entirely and, unlike Greek/Norse, never got a `colored/` companion
set; this sheet regenerates the eight bronze plates from the canon in
`Database/symbolism.json` so the owner can drop fresh renders straight
over the existing files, and adds the eight colored plates the theme
was missing.

**Border identity:** a bronze ring carved as a continuous **rodovik
solar-wheel (kolovrat) carving**, running clockwise, broken every
quarter turn by a **wheat-sheaf roundel** cradling the day's planetary
glyph — distinct from every other bronze theme's border (no meander,
no knotwork, no hieroglyphs).

**Style note:** aged bronze relief is the base register because this
is the metal-swap family — gold and silver variants hue-swap the same
warm bronze pixels programmatically, so no separate gold/silver
prompts are needed here.
