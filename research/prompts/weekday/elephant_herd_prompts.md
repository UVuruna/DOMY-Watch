# Elephant Herd — the 15th Weekday Theme (Gemini)

The third animal society, closing a deliberate trio — three themes,
three different principles of order:

- **Wolf pack** — hierarchy by RANK (Alpha to Omega)
- **Bee hive** — caste by FUNCTION AND AGE (the career is the clock)
- **Elephant herd** — matriarchy by MEMORY (the leader is the one
  who remembers)

The herd's whole claim on a CLOCK is memory: the matriarch leads
because she remembers — where water hid in the drought of thirty
years ago, which pass is safe, which scent means poachers. Memory is
time held in a living body, and Saturn's day gets its purest form:
an elephant dies when its sixth and last set of molars wears out —
**the teeth are the animal's own hourglass**, and the old ones know
where the water sleeps.

| Sat ♄ | **Elder** | the ancient cow on her last molars, digging a well in the dry riverbed because she REMEMBERS the water — time in the teeth, memory as inheritance |

**The dual, said plainly:** elephants demonstrably return to the
bones of their dead and stand over them, touching the skulls with
their trunks — documented, not folklore. So the Sunday pair here is
not ruler/servant but **ruler/remembered ruler**: the living
matriarch resting her trunk on the skull of the one who taught her
the map, under the eclipsed sun. Glory and Eclipse in its oldest
form — the throne and the grave of the throne. (If the owner wants
the softer servant-face instead, the fallback is the Allomother —
say so and the sheet reshuffles Monday.)

## Style — the Sixth Metal Theme

Weathered **bronze sculptural relief on dark cracked stone**, the
house material, hue-swap safe; plus `colored/` and `dual/`.

**Border language (collides with nothing):** a bronze ring carved
with **acacia thorn branches interlaced with paired crescent
tusks**, and four small roundels bearing the day's planetary glyph.

**Family resemblance of the duals:** the Memory plate carries the
thin eclipsed-sun corona ring in its sky, the mark of every dual.

**Metal-swap note (technical):** warm bronze pixels (hue ~10–60°)
swap to gold/silver. Keep non-metal accents COOL or RED (moonlight,
ember, dust red — below the window); the gilded sun disc behind the
Matriarch and the corona ring on Memory swap WITH the metal by
design.

**Colored arc — the seasons of water:** where the wolves walk the
hours and the bees walk out of the hive, the elephants walk the
YEAR: golden dawn (Sun), moonlit night (Moon), red dust storm
(Mars), violet dusk — the hour infrasound carries farthest (Mercury),
clear noon at the full river (Jupiter), green wet-season reunion
(Venus), white drought at the dry riverbed (Saturn). Memory is
tested by drought; the colored Saturday shows the test.
