# Professions — the Seven Callings Weekday Theme (bronze plates)

The Professions set — Ruler, Physician, Soldier, Merchant, Priest,
Artist, Farmer, plus the Sunday dual Servant — predates the
prompt-sheet convention entirely; this sheet regenerates all eight
plates from the canon in `Database/symbolism.json`
(`articles.profession.<body>.base` / `.faces`) so the owner can drop
fresh renders straight over the existing files. Menu title:
`WEEKDAY_THEME_TITLES["profession"] = "Professions"`.

**Border identity:** each calling keeps its OWN canonical guild emblem
stitched into the border, straight from its own canon paragraph —
Physician's serpent-and-laurel wreath (Asclepius' rod), Soldier's oak
leaves (endurance, not triumph), Merchant's coin-and-scale medallions,
Priest's ring of open books, Farmer's braided wheat, all explicit in
the article text; Ruler gets an eagle-crest rim and Artist a
mask-and-lyre rim, extrapolated from the imperial-eagle and
comedy/tragedy-mask imagery already inside their own scenes. The seven
read as one set through shared bronze relief and register, not through
a borrowed ring.

**Style note:** weathered bronze sculptural relief on dark cracked
stone is the base register — the SAME material family as Greek, Norse
and Wolf Pack, so the existing gold/silver hue-swap keeps working
unchanged on this set too. This sheet now covers BOTH registers: the
base bronze relief plates below, and the full-color `colored/` set
consolidated into its own section further down (moved verbatim from
`colored_badge_prompts.md` — owner consolidation 2026-07-15: each
theme's sheet should carry everything it needs, no hunting across
files for a theme's colored plates).

Files land in `assets/weekday/profession/primary/`. Stems below are the exact
Capitalized names from `WEEKDAY_THEME_FILES["profession"]` — note the
Sunday dual is `Servant.png`, sitting DIRECTLY in the folder, NOT under
a `dual/` subfolder, per `WEEKDAY_DUAL_FILES["profession"] =
"profession/Servant"`.

## The Ninth — The Polymath (`Polymath.png`)

**Doctrine (`pantheon_catalog.md`, owner round four 2026-07-15):** the
Polymath is the Ninth of the Professions set, LOCKED — *"ne može
klovn da bude najviši na piramidi"* ("a jester cannot be the highest
on the pyramid"). This is the Union form of the Ninth, not the
Excluded form: the Leonardo archetype, the estates of all seven
callings held inside ONE person rather than mocked from outside them —
and the Union law is absolute across every theme: **the Ninth must
never rank below the seated figures.** One quiet detail keeps the
retired Jester's own virtue alive inside the new Ninth, per the same
doctrine note ("the Jester retires to the Wider lane, his
truth-to-the-throne line surviving inside the Polymath's article") —
a single small bell worked into his cuff, the last trace of the only
figure ever allowed to speak truth to the throne.

