# Slavic Months Prompts (Gemini) — the Wheel of Labour

The CALENDAR-POINTER 12-set (owner-sealed R7b round, 2026-07-21,
`config.defaults.SLAVIC_MONTHS`): the twelve Croatian months as proper
nouns, one plate per Gregorian month, meant to MOUNT on the Calendar
pointer at `CALENDAR_MOUNT_RADIUS_FRACTION` (60-70% of the dial
radius, the DESIGN ZODIAC law — "Zodiac i sve što ima 12 TREBA da bude
moguće da se AKTIVIRA na CALENDAR POINTER") beside the zodiac signs
and the Chinese animals it already reads. Each article
(`Database/encyclopedia.json` → `months.<Croatian name>`) argues the
month from its OWN etymology — not a god, not a number, but WHAT THE
LAND WAS DOING — and every plate below draws exactly that labour or
that piece of nature, never the name itself (the house rule: no
lettering, ever).

**Naming (R7b, `defaults.SLAVIC_MONTHS`):** `(croatian proper noun,
english gloss, ascii stem, gregorian month)` — the CROATIAN name is
the proper noun the dial shows, the GLOSS is the plain-English meaning
folded into this sheet's own heading per entry, the STEM is the
ASCII plate filename. Twelve entries, Siječanj (January) through
Prosinac (December).

**Register:** the house night-window family — the SAME stained-glass
register as the era emblems (see [Era Prompts](../era/era_prompts.md))
and the archetype lancets, so the months sit beside the year's other
turning-point badges without a style break. ROUND rose-window
medallions, one unifying device across all twelve: a SPOKED CART-WHEEL
leadwork rim (the set's own title, "the Wheel of Labour" — kolo,
'wheel', is literally August's own name, Kolovoz) — twelve slim
spokes radiating from the rim toward the glass, exactly like the era
emblems' twelve-ray sundial rim is THEIR shared device. Each window's
field color reads its own season (deep winter blue-white sliding
through green spring, gold summer, russet autumn and back to winter
blue) so the twelve read as one wheel turning at a glance, the same
"one set, one story" law the era emblems keep.

**Drop paths:** `assets/months/` — `Sijecanj.png`, `Veljaca.png`,
`Ozujak.png`, `Travanj.png`, `Svibanj.png`, `Lipanj.png`,
`Srpanj.png`, `Kolovoz.png`, `Rujan.png`, `Listopad.png`,
`Studeni.png`, `Prosinac.png`. NOTE: `MONTHS_ART_DIR` is a CANONICAL
SOURCELESS root (owner precedent, the subdial sets) — deliberately
OUTSIDE `constants.ART_SOURCED_ROOTS`, no `<source>` split, ONE file
per month regardless of which art source (Gemini/ChatGPT) is active.

**House rules carried from every other sheet:** photorealistic
render, isolated background (no transparency-checkerboard artifact),
the circular window shape IS the frame, NO lettering anywhere in any
image.

## The twelve months

the woodcutter's month) → `assets/months/Sijecanj.png`

```
ROUND rose-window stained-glass medallion, night-window register, photorealistic render, isolated background, the circular window shape IS the frame. Deep frost-blue and iron-grey glass throughout, the coldest window of the twelve. Center: a woodcutter's axe buried mid-swing in a felled log, fresh pale wood showing bright against the bark, a small stacked cord of cut timber beside it on hard-frozen, sledge-rutted ground; bare black trees stand close around, the sap long gone down for the cold. Border: the shared spoked cart-wheel leadwork rim, frost-rimed and dim. Palette: frost blue, iron grey, pale cut-wood gold, dark-iron lead. NO lettering anywhere.
```
