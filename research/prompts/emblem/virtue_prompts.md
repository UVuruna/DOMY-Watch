# Virtue Prompts (Gemini)

The eight **Virtues** — the VIRTUES weekday theme's own complete
sheet, drawn straight from the cross-cure canon in `SYMBOLISM.md`.
Allegorical logos, not portraits. Paste one prompt at a time;
generate all 8 in one chat session so the set stays consistent.

**Sunday is double.** The center carries the Ruler (bright face,
Justice) and the Servant (dark face, Humility — the theme's Sunday
DUAL) as two separate entries — 8 items over 7 days.

Drop location: `assets/emblem/virtue/<Name>.png` — Justice, Humility,
Serenity, Courage, Wisdom, Generosity, Love, Patience.

Sibling families (each has its own sheet): [Sins](sin_prompts.md),
[Moods](mood_prompts.md). Each row lines up across the three sets on
its cross-cure axis (Tuesday: Courage cures Fear, Wrath is Courage's
own shadow, Zeal is the hour it burns in) — a sanity check while
generating.

## Design Note — the Family Device

All badges keep the house circular-badge shape from
[Profession Prompts](../weekday/profession_prompts.md)'s colored
plates; the item's accent color is its day's arm color. The VIRTUE
family device, held fixed across all 8: an engraved **sunburst**
field — fine rays radiating outward, tinted the day's color — inside
a polished **gold** ring with a laurel-leaf wreath motif (ascending,
radiant, outward-giving; gold is already the Sun's metal on this
dial).

> **RESOLVED (owner verdict 2026-07-12).** The crown-and-basin
> composition above IS the canon Humility — the owner generated and
> compared a wheat-emblem v2 and rejected it ("the bowed wheat reads
> as famine and drought, like the plagues on Egypt, not humility");
> the Christ-like figure lifting the crown off and setting a foot in
> the water stays, and its kinship with the Servant plate is accepted
> as the Sunday family resemblance. No regeneration.
