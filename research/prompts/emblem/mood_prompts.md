# Mood Prompts (Gemini)

The eight **Moods** (the color-mood table) — the MOODS weekday
theme's own complete sheet, drawn straight from the cross-cure canon
in `SYMBOLISM.md`. Allegorical logos, not portraits. Paste one prompt
at a time; generate all 8 in one chat session so the set stays
consistent.

**Sunday is double.** The center carries the Ruler's mood (Glory,
bright face) and the Servant's mood (Awe, dark face — the theme's
Sunday DUAL) as two separate entries — 8 items over 7 days. The theme
also owns a NINTH (see the bottom): **The Ninth Mood** — the Eclipse
plate, the event mood outside the hours.

Drop location: `assets/emblem/mood/<Name>.png` — Glory, Awe (rename
2026-07-14 — formerly `Eclipse.png`; the code rename maps the file,
and the existing Eclipse plate stays valid art until the new Awe
plate lands), Calm, Zeal, Sorrow, Joy, Passion, Renewal — plus
`Eclipse.png` as the Ninth Mood's own plate.

Sibling families (each has its own sheet):
[Virtues](virtue_prompts.md), [Sins](sin_prompts.md). Each row lines
up across the three sets on its cross-cure axis (Tuesday: Courage
cures Fear, Wrath is Courage's own shadow, Zeal is the hour it burns
in).

## Design Note — the Family Device

House circular-badge shape; accent color = the day's arm color. The
MOOD family device, fixed across all items: a **gradient sky-disc**
field — the hour's own light washing from rim to center — inside a
polished **silver** ring with a sundial hour-tick motif (atmospheric,
temporal, the hour made visible; silver because moods are the Moon's
domain — the hour's changing light, not a fixed moral stance).

## The Ninth — The Ninth Mood (Eclipse)

Eight moods share the day between them; the NINTH is the event mood
OUTSIDE the hours (owner 8+1 doctrine) — the moment the Servant
crosses the Ruler, the scale's two triangles balancing at zero. The
plate below is its canon art (`Eclipse.png` — it remains the Ninth
Mood's own plate; it is no longer the Sunday dual's name, which is
Awe above).
