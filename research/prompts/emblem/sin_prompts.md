# Sin Prompts (Gemini)

The eight **Sins** (the arm vices) — the SINS weekday theme's own
complete sheet, drawn straight from the cross-cure canon in
`SYMBOLISM.md`. Allegorical logos, not portraits. Paste one prompt at
a time; generate all 8 in one chat session so the set stays
consistent.

**Sunday is double.** The center carries the Ruler's vice (Pride,
bright face) and the Servant's vice (Servility, dark face — the
theme's Sunday DUAL) as two separate entries — 8 items over 7 days.

Drop location: `assets/emblem/sin/<Name>.png` — Pride, Servility,
Fear, Wrath, Greed, Excess, Jealousy, Envy.

Sibling families (each has its own sheet):
[Virtues](virtue_prompts.md), [Moods](mood_prompts.md). Each row
lines up across the three sets on its cross-cure axis (Tuesday:
Courage cures Fear, Wrath is Courage's own shadow, Zeal is the hour
it burns in).

| Friday | red | Jealousy |

## Design Note — the Family Device

House circular-badge shape; accent color = the day's arm color. The
SIN family device, fixed across all 8: a **fissured obsidian** field
— jagged cracks glowing the day's color like magma beneath black
stone — inside a blackened **iron** ring with a thorned-bramble motif
(descending, coiled, shadow leaking through; iron because sin is the
one family with no dial metal of its own — corrosion, not alchemy).
