# Calendar Art Prompts (Gemini) — the Almanac's Twelve Month Medallions

The CALENDAR pointer's art needs (owner canon 2026-07-16, canon in
[The DOMY Canon](../../../CANON.md), §The Calendar). The Calendar
divides the 24h dial into twelve 2-hour wedges and offers two wheels
in the Paint/Light slot — **Zodiac** (left) and **Almanac** (right).

**The wedges themselves carry NO art — they are FLAT COLOR.** Owner
display doctrine: the wedges are too small for medallions that would
not touch, so a wedge simply LIGHTS by raising its opacity, and the
pointer uses the PINNED slot layout for whatever the user seats.
Nothing is generated for the wedges. State this plainly to anyone
briefing generation.

**What IS needed: twelve MONTH MEDALLIONS** for the Almanac's
hover/encyclopedia articles — a round medallion per month, each in
its month HUE from CANON, carrying that month's seasonal/civil
imagery, with ONE consistent border shared across all twelve so the
set reads as one wheel.

**Register:** the round stained-glass rondel of the DOMY house family
(the same night-window glass as the archetype sheets — see
[Trinity Prompts](trinity_prompts.md)), so the Almanac articles sit
beside the pointer archetypes without a style break. Each month's
glass is dominated by that month's CANON hue; the leadwork border is
IDENTICAL on all twelve (a wheel-and-tick rim — a plain circular band
carrying twelve small ring-ticks, echoing the pointer's tick=day
reading).

**Month hues (CANON §The Month Dozen):** January `#8000FF`,
February `#0000FF`, March `#0080FF`, April `#00FFFF`, May `#00FF80`,
June `#00FF00`, July `#80FF00`, August `#FFFF00`, September `#FFBF00`,
October `#FF0000`, November `#FF0080`, December `#FF00FF`.

**Drop paths:** `assets/archetype/<source>/calendar/` —
`January.png` … `December.png`.
