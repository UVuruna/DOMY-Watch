# Family Archetype Prompts (Gemini) — the Household Triangle and the Hearth

The TRINITY pointer's LIGHT archetype (owner doctrine 2026-07-16, canon
in [The DOMY Canon](../../../CANON.md)): where the paint trio carries the
heavenly courtroom, the light trio carries the HOME — the three arms are
the three members of the family, and the HEARTH — the union of the three
— sits at the CENTER. Owner's image brief, verbatim intent:

- **the Child / the Dawn** — new, growing, unformed; he stands at the
  TOP and the two below STRIVE FOR him: the **Dawn**.
- **the Father / the Shield** — trust and calm, the guardian's color,
  the pillar of the house behind whom the smallest sleeps soundest:
  the **Shield**.
- **the Mother / the Heart** — love embodied, the heart of the house,
  the one who carries everyone and holds the household together:
  the **Heart**.
- **the Hearth** — the family's union, the fire that warms, seated in
  the middle.

The doctrine the articles argue from (never inscribed in the art): the
family is a love CIRCUIT, not a pool — the Child loves the Father, the
Father loves the Mother, the Mother loves the Child, so the given love
CIRCULATES and no one is left dry. That circuit runs
COUNTER-CLOCKWISE: **the world's day runs clockwise, the home runs
against the clock** — love in a family flows against time, the parents
pouring into the future and the child returning it to the past. The
apex mirrors the paint trio: in PAINT the top is the one who JUDGES,
in LIGHT the top is the one who is SERVED — the same triangle, once as
the hierarchy of power, once as the hierarchy of care. The Hesburgh
anchor — *"the most important thing a father can do for his children
is to love their mother"* — and *One Flesh* (Genesis 2:24) belong to
the ARTICLES and the UI; the house rule stands: images never carry
lettering.

**Register:** the stained-glass night-window family — the same register
as the Trinity paint glass and the Scale glass (see
[Trinity Prompts](trinity_prompts.md)); the three members are LANCET
windows in their arm's LIGHT color (green top 12h / light blue 04h /
light red 20h), the Hearth is a ROUND rosette like the Eye and the
Union — but where the Eye WATCHES with a clear iris, the Hearth WARMS
with a living fire. A metal-cameo register can be added later if the
owner wants a second look.

**the Hearth** → `family/Hearth.png`

watching.)*

```
ROUND rose window — a circular stained-glass rosette, night-window register, photorealistic render, isolated background. Center: a warm living FIRE burning in a round stone hearth at the heart of the window — golden and rose flames rising, embers glowing amber, the single warmest and brightest point of the glass, real firelight radiating outward. Three broad ribbons of glass braid INTO the fire from the rim in the three family colors — light blue descending from one side, light red from another, spring green from the top — twisting together into the flame at the center so the three feed one fire. Straight rays of warm gold glass burst from the hearth through rings of soft rose cloudglass to the rim. Rim: gothic stone tracery warmed by the firelight; three rim roundels evenly placed — a kite shield in light-blue glass, a glowing heart in light-red glass, a rising sun in green glass. Palette: warm gold and amber firelight, rose glow, light blue, light red, spring green, dark warm-stone lead. NO lettering anywhere.
```
