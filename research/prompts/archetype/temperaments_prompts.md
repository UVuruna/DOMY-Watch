# Seasons Archetype Prompts (Gemini) — the Four Temperaments

The SEASONS pointer's archetype (owner doctrine 2026-07-16, canon in
[The DOMY Canon](../../../CANON.md)): the oldest four-fold reading of
man there is — Hippocrates' four humors — laid on the four season arms
in their classical correspondence, on the seasons palette's OWN colors.
The Seasons DO split by wheel now (owner seal 2026-07-17, CANON
§Seasons light): PAINT = these Four Temperaments; LIGHT = the
Tetramorph on the Four Elements wheel (its section below); both share
the ONE Throne center. Each window personifies one temperament — its
humor, its element, its person-type (the way the Trinity has its
syndromes), a bright and a dark face, and its age of man. Owner's
image brief, verbatim intent:

- **Sanguine** — spring, GREEN (`#129412`): blood · air; the Companion,
  quick to love and laugh; openness and joy / fickleness; **childhood
  & youth**.
- **Choleric** — summer, YELLOW (`#D9D900`): yellow bile · fire; the
  Commander, the fire that drives; drive and decisiveness / wrath and
  tyranny; **the prime**.
- **Melancholic** — autumn, RED (`#D4330F`): black bile · earth; the
  Thinker, depth bought with heaviness; depth and precision / brooding
  and despair; **middle age**.
- **Phlegmatic** — winter, BLUE (`#0A70D8`): phlegm · water; the
  Peacemaker, the calm that outlasts every storm; serenity and mercy /
  sloth and passivity; **old age**.

The doctrine the articles argue from (never inscribed in the art): the
two axes are TOTAL opposites in the humor square, exactly the dial's
diagonal logic — **Sanguine–Melancholic** (spring against autumn,
laughter against heaviness, the morning of life against its evening)
and **Choleric–Phlegmatic** (summer against winter, fire against
water, the only pair opposed in BOTH qualities). The owner's poem and
the humor-square anchors belong to the ARTICLES and the UI; the house
rule stands: images never carry lettering.

**Register:** the stained-glass night-window family — the same register
as the Trinity and Family glass (see [Trinity Prompts](trinity_prompts.md)).
Window shape: **TALL pointed-arch LANCET windows**, one per
temperament, each a full seasonal world with its person at the center;
the arm's season color floods the whole glass so the four read as a
single set at a glance. The bright and dark faces of each temperament
share the ONE window (the two-row article carries the split); a
metal-cameo register can be added later if the owner wants a second
look.

**Drop paths:** `assets/archetype/<source>/temperaments/` —
`Sanguine.png`, `Choleric.png`, `Melancholic.png`, `Phlegmatic.png`;
