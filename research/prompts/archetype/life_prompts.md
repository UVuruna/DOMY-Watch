# Life Archetype Prompts (Gemini) — the Eight Ages, the Wheel of a Life

The COMPASS pointer's LIGHT archetype (owner canon 2026-07-16, canon
in [The DOMY Canon](../../../CANON.md)): where PAINT carries the
world's estates (see [Walks Prompts](walks_prompts.md)), LIGHT
carries a single human LIFE — the day as a lifetime, sunrise to
sunrise, eight ages seated on the light palette's own hues. The
owner's own poem (day, evening, night and morning) belongs to these
articles; text pending, he delivers it when ready.

**The wheel CLOSES.** After the midnight silver and the predawn mist,
dawn births again — **NOVUS ORDO SECLORUM enacted on the dial**, the
order of ages born anew with every turn. Quote anchors for the
articles (never inscribed in the art): Ecclesiastes 3 — *"a time to
be born, and a time to die"* — and Shakespeare's seven ages —
*"all the world's a stage"* (As You Like It) — to whose stage we
give the eighth player: the one not yet on it, the Unborn.

The eight ages and their hues (CANON §Compass light):

| Hour | Age | Hue |
|---|---|---|
| 03h | **the Unborn** — ancestors and the not-yet-born | predawn mist `#8FA8C8` |
| 06h | **Birth** — first light | pale rose `#FFD9CC` |
| 09h | **Childhood** — morning | spring green `#7CE577` |
| 12h | **Youth** — the zenith | sun yellow `#FFE800` |
| 15h | **Maturity** — afternoon | amber `#FFB400` |
| 18h | **the Elder** — the harvest of a life | ember `#FF6A3C` |
| 21h | **Old age** — evening | dusk violet `#9C6BD4` |
| 24h | **Death** — midnight (never black; light finds the Moon) | moonlight silver `#C8D7F0` |

**The image layer — TWO registers, owner picks (or runs both).** The
owner asked for living beings that symbolize each period of a life;
two options are written in full below so he can choose or generate
them in parallel. Both keep the stained-glass night-window family
(the register of the Trinity and Walks glass); each age is a TALL
LANCET window in that age's light hue.

**Drop paths:**

tree (Yggdrasil is a Ninth). The seed at the root of the last window
