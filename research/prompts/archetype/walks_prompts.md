# Walks Archetype Prompts (Gemini) — the Eight Walks of Life

The COMPASS pointer's PAINT archetype (owner canon 2026-07-16, canon
in [The DOMY Canon](../../../CANON.md)): the eight arms carry the
eight ESTATES of society, seated POSITIONALLY — each justified by its
HOUR, since the colors are already moods of the day (the throne at
noon, the guard at sunset, the lamplit student in deep night, the
plough at dawn). Paint carries the WORLD's order: estates are MATTER,
so each window glazes in its estate's own substance — crown gold,
coin copper, iron and blood, stage velvet, road dust, lamp ink,
field earth, alb linen.

The four oppositions the articles argue from (the diagonals, never
inscribed in the art):

- **King–Wanderer** (12h–24h) — the crown against the open road:
  Pride and Humility again, power at its zenith against the man who
  owns nothing but the way under his feet.
- **Soldier–Farmer** (18h–06h) — the sword against the plough:
  Isaiah 2:4, *"they shall beat their swords into plowshares"* — the
  frontier hour against first light.
- **Merchant–Scholar** (15h–03h) — what a thing COSTS against what
  it is WORTH: the closed deal against the lamplit question.
- **Artist–Priest** (21h–09h) — the stage against the altar: two
  performances, both reaching past this world.

**Register:** the stained-glass night-window family — the same
register as the Trinity glass (see
[Trinity Prompts](trinity_prompts.md)); each estate is a TALL LANCET
window in its material color, the eight forming the octa wheel. A
metal-cameo register can be added later if the owner wants a second
look.
