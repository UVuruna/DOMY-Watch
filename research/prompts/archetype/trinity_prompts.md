# Trinity Archetype Prompts (Gemini) — the Courtroom Trio and the Eye

The TRINITY pointer's archetype set (owner doctrine 2026-07-16, canon
in [The DOMY Canon](../../../CANON.md)): the three arms carry the
three persons of the heavenly courtroom, and the ALL-SEEING EYE — the
union of the three — sits at the CENTER. Owner's image brief,
verbatim intent:

- **Jesus** — the one who helps others, who always steps in to
  DEFEND the other — which he was: the **Advocate**.
- **the Devil** — the one who wants to punish, who ENJOYS punishing
  others: the **Prosecutor**.
- **The One (God)** — the judge, the one who knows all and sees all:
  the **Judge**.
- **The Eye** — the all-seeing eye is the UNION of the three
  members, seated in the middle.

The scriptural anchors the articles will argue from (never inscribed
in the art): Jesus is literally called the Advocate — *Parakletos*,
1 John 2:1 — and Satan literally the Accuser — *Kategoros*,
Revelation 12:10; Job 1–2 and Zechariah 3 stage heaven AS a
courtroom. The mottos **E PLURIBUS UNUM** (The One) and **ANNUIT
COEPTIS** (the Eye) belong to the ARTICLES and the UI — the house
rule stands: images never carry lettering.

**Register:** the stained-glass night-window family — the same
register as the Scale glass (see
[Scale Prompts](../badge/scale_badge_prompts.md)); the three persons
are LANCET windows in their arm's color (blue 04h / red 20h / gold
12h), the Eye is a ROUND rosette like the Union. The Lucifer master
already carries an all-seeing-eye roundel at its apex — this set
makes that eye a window of its own. A metal-cameo register can be
added later if the owner wants a second look.

**Drop paths:** `assets/archetype/<source>/trinity/` —
`Jesus_Advocate.png`, `Devil_Prosecutor.png`, `One_Judge.png`,
