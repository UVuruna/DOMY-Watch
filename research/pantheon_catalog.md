# The Pantheon Catalog — Both Rosters, Seat by Seat

**Purpose (owner 2026-07-15):** the review document — every theme,
every seat, BOTH modes side by side: **Planetary** (the shipped
roster, seated as day-ruler counterparts) and **Pantheon** (the new
roster, seated by the figure's REAL rank and role in its own
mythology, bound to our seat archetypes). Deliberately more than
needed: alternatives, pros and cons, and every open dilemma flagged
`⚖` — we decide those together. Option names APPROVED: **Pantheon /
Planetary** (SR Panteon / Planetarni), default Planetary.

## Greek

| Seat | Planetary | Pantheon | Status |
|---|---|---|---|

| moon | Selene | **Poseidon** | firm |
| mars | Ares | **Ares** ✓ | ⚖ vs Artemis |
| mercury | Hermes | **Athena** | firm |
| jupiter | Zeus | **Apollo** | firm |
| venus | Aphrodite | **Aphrodite** ✓ | firm |

| dual | Helios / Phaethon | Zeus / **Prometheus** | ⚖ open |
| ninth | Hades | Hades (both modes) | doctrine |

**Sunday — Zeus.** The sovereign, full stop. Justice is the king's
virtue, Pride his recorded sin (the myths are a catalog of it),
Glory his weather. No serious alternative.

**Monday — Poseidon.** The owner's own call and the strongest forged
link in the set: the blue seat is the SEA's — Serenity is calm water,
Fear is what the sea becomes, and the Moon literally drags his tides.
Alternative considered: Artemis (Selene-conflation) — rejected: it
repeats the "literal counterpart" mistake the Pantheon exists to fix.

**⚖ Tuesday — Ares, challenged by Artemis.** The open Greek question.
- *For Ares:* he IS both faces of the seat — Courage and Wrath in one
  body; world-famous; the plate and article already exist (Planetary
  reuse, zero cost). Against: among the gods he is the despised one —
  fame yes, dignity arguable.
- *For Artemis:* the hunt is a soldier's craft — stalking, the drawn
  bow, the kill; she is fierce, orange-blooded (torches, the wild).
  Against: her wrath stories are punitive, not martial; and it
  orphans Ares from a roster built on fame.
- **The owner's counter-idea — the hunt is GREEN, not orange:**
  hunting as the woodland twin of agriculture (both feed the house,
  both wait on the land). That reading sends Artemis to SATURDAY —
  see below. My honest position: the hunt's *temperament* is orange
  (Zeal, the chase, the strike) while its *provenance* is green (the
  forest, the season, the patience of the stalk) — both readings are
  defensible, which is exactly why this seat is flagged ⚖.

**Wednesday — Athena.** Wisdom is the seat's own word — the goddess
of the seat's virtue, strategy as applied intellect, the owl as the
purple night-mind. Cost: Hermes yields (he keeps his Planetary seat).
Alternative: keep Hermes (fame, trade=Merchant estate) — but then
Athena, the second-most-important Olympian, has NO seat, which is the
exact failure the owner named.

**Thursday — Apollo.** Delphi makes him the Priest estate incarnate;
prophecy and music given freely (Generosity), plague-arrows and
hubris-punishment when the yellow overflows (Excess). Alternative:
Dionysos (Excess personified, Joy's god) — colorful but seats the
VICE, not the figure's rank; Apollo outranks him on fame and breadth.

**Friday — Aphrodite ✓.** Love and Jealousy complete. No challenge.

**⚖ Saturday — Demeter, with the Artemis variant.**
- *For Demeter:* green, grain, the Patience of the harvest, Renewal =
  Persephone's return — the seat's whole bundle in one myth; and the
  week ends in the field the way Saturn's day always did.
- *For Artemis-at-Saturday (the owner's hunting-is-green reading):*
  the forest as the older field; the huntress as the green provider;
  Renewal as the game's seasonal return. Against: Patience and Envy
  sit oddly on her; Demeter's claim on green is direct where
  Artemis's is poetic.
- **Recommendation:** Demeter at Saturday, Ares keeps Tuesday, and
  Artemis leads the Wider-Pantheon lane — unless the owner prefers
  the huntress seated, in which case Tuesday (temperament) is the
  stronger of her two chairs.

**⚖ Dual (Sunday's Servant face).** Zeus needs a servant twin the way
Helios has Phaethon. Proposal: **Prometheus** — the giver chained for
giving, the servant of mankind against the throne; thematically
perfect, but he is a Titan, not Zeus's own face. Alternatives:
Zeus-Xenios (the beggar-guise protector of guests — same body, two
faces, structurally cleanest) or Ganymede (the cup-bearer — weakest).
Owner picks.

**Sunday — Ra ✓.** The one throne where supreme and solar agree.
The Afu-Ra night-barque dual serves both rosters unchanged.

**Monday — Isis.** The healer who reassembled Osiris — the Physician
archetype embodied; her magic is Monday's blue calm; arguably the
most venerated Egyptian deity. ⚖ *Placement question, not membership:*
Isis could claim FRIDAY instead (the loving wife as Love's seat),
which would return Khonsu to Monday and keep Hathor out. My
recommendation stands — Isis's defining act is the healing, Hathor's
is the music: Monday-Isis + Friday-Hathor uses both at full strength.

**Tuesday — Horus.** The avenger's war — Courage with a cause, the
falcon the world knows. Montu's only claim was the literal one.

**Wednesday — Thoth ✓.** Wisdom's own scribe, unchallenged.

**Thursday — Anubis (the owner's call, seconded).** "If Amun is in,
Anubis cannot be out" — agreed, and the seat argument holds: the
rite-master who performs the passage IS the Priest estate; the
weighing of the heart is Thursday's Generosity ledger read from the
other side. Amun (the hidden temple god — the priest's PRIEST) moves
to the Wider-Pantheon lane; his fame outside Egyptology is thinner
than Anubis's everywhere else.

**Saturday — Osiris ✓.** Grain, death, Renewal — the seat's story.

**Sunday — Odin.** The Allfather; Pride that hanged itself on the
tree for wisdom — both faces in one figure. Dual proposal: **Odin the
Wanderer** — the one-eyed beggar at the door is the Servant face of
the same body (the cleanest dual structure in the whole catalog).

**⚖ Monday — Hel, against Njord and Frigg.**
- *For Hel:* Monday's dark face is FEAR, and she is its throne; the
  half-dead queen keeps the stillest Calm; hugely famous today.
- *For Njord:* the sea god — the same blue-water logic that seats
  Poseidon; but his fame is a shadow of Hel's.
- *For Frigg:* the mother who tried to heal fate itself (the
  mistletoe oaths) — the Physician reading; quieter fame.
- **Recommendation:** Hel — the seat's shadow face is a legitimate
  claim (Ares holds Tuesday the same way) and the Poseidon logic is
  already used once; twice would look like a formula.

**Tuesday — Thor.** The fighter the world knows; Courage, Zeal, and
enough Wrath stories to fill the vice. Tyr's courage (the hand in the
wolf's mouth) is nobler — but it is ONE act, and Thursday now honors
it better.

**Wednesday — Loki.** The cunning purple mind; Greed/trickery is the
seat's vice and his résumé. Odin vacates for the throne.

**Thursday — Tyr.** The oath-keeper who GAVE his hand — Generosity by
sacrifice, the priest of law. Alternative: Heimdall (the watchman,
Gjallarhorn as the temple horn) — thinner claim, Wider-Pantheon lane.

**Saturday — Freyr.** THE harvest god — green Patience, the field's
Renewal; ends the week the way Demeter does for Greece.

**Sunday — Perun / Monday — Veles.** The supreme thunderer takes the
throne and the great duality maps onto the dial's own axis: storm
above, water and the under-world below — Veles on the blue seat is
the Slavic Poseidon move, and here it is ALSO the culture's central
myth (the Perun–Veles combat), which makes it firm.

**⚖ Wednesday — Svarog (soft).** The smith as the making MIND —
craft as Wednesday's wisdom. Honest doubt: a forge is not obviously
purple, and Veles (the native trickster-mind) would fit the seat
better but is spent on Monday. Alternative: leave Wednesday to
**Hors** (planetary reuse) and give Svarog the Wider-Pantheon lane —
but then the Slavic Pantheon adds only two new figures and the owner
explicitly wanted Svarog IN. Recommendation: Svarog, acknowledged as
the least-forged link in this catalog.

**Thursday — Dažbog.** *Dažd-bog* — literally "the god who gives":
Thursday's Generosity by NAME. The strongest single-word claim in
the entire document.

**⚖ Friday — Lada vs Mokoš.**
- *For Lada:* the folk-songs' love goddess — Love's seat by name and
  wedding custom. Scholarly caveat: her ancient attestation is
  debated (possibly a folklore back-formation).
- *For Mokoš:* unquestionably ancient and major, but she is fate,
  moisture and women's WORK — Wednesday/Saturday energies more than
  red Friday passion. **Recommendation:** Lada, with the caveat
  stated; if the owner wants only attested deities, keep Mokoš ✓.

**⚖ Dual.** Perun's Servant face has no obvious native twin.
Options: keep the theme's existing Old Dažbog dual serving both
rosters (cheapest, and the winter-sun elder reads as ANY sun's
servant face), or a Perun-specific "Quiet Perun" (the idol asleep
until spring thunder). Recommendation: share Old Dažbog.

The audit found something important: **Omega is not missing — Omega
is already the Sunday DUAL** (`wolf/primary/dual/omega.png`, the
Alpha & Omega pair — scripture-grade symbolism already in the dial).
So the famous ladder completes with **Gamma** (the enforcer-hunter)
and **Delta** (the runner-messenger) taking Tuesday and Wednesday,

(the breeding pair IS the pack's love seat, biologically famous) or
reseat **Omega** there — which would cost the Alpha/Omega dual its
partner and need a new Servant face. **Recommendation: keep Mate,
keep the Alpha/Omega dual.**

## Themes argued UNCHANGED (both modes identical)

- **Bee / Elephant:** Queen–Drone and Matriarch–Musth are already the
  documentary-famous vocabulary; the worker castes (Nurse, Guard,
  Builder, Forager) are the biology itself.
- **Professions:** they ARE the archetype definitions.
- **Creeds / Mysteries / Bible ×3:** seated by our symbolism from the
  start; Bible already ships three rosters — the living proof of the
  Pantheon/Planetary mechanism.
- **Alchemy, Japanese week, Planet signs, Planets, Cosmos:** closed
  or literal sets; Cosmos candidates are endless but its seven +
  Big Bang ninth already carry the celestial canon.
- **Zodiac / Chinese:** fixed twelves + their thirteenths (Ophiuchus,
  the Cat) — nothing to reseat.
