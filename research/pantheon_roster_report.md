# The Two Rosters — Seats Are Archetypes, Fame Comes First

> **SUPERSEDED for roster contents (2026-07-15):** the per-theme
> tables below predate the final verdicts — the LOCKED rosters,
> duals and ninths live in [The Pantheon Catalog](pantheon_catalog.md)
> and only there. This file remains as the doctrine's origin story.

**Owner doctrine (2026-07-15, verbatim direction):** a seat is NOT a
planet looking for its literal counterpart. A seat is OUR archetype —
a color, a virtue, a vice, a mood, an estate — and the imperative is
that the seat holds a figure **everyone knows**, connected to that
bundle by symbolism WE forge. *"Mi smo ti koji ćemo naći povezanosti —
nećemo insistirati na povezanostima koje su drugi dali."* The supreme
god of a mythology takes the supreme seat; an obscure god never wins
a seat just because he is somebody's "literal pandan". The existing
day-fit rosters STAY as the second option — nothing ships out.

This file supersedes the first draft of itself and the two Haiku root
reports. The audit facts from that pass survive (Thoth, Ra, Morana,
Mokoš, the Physician were never missing; Hades, Baldur, Set and
Crnobog hold the NINTH seats and never join a seven).

A pantheon seat is argued from THIS row — never from "who pulled the
sun chariot". The strongest claims bind BOTH faces of a day (its
virtue and its vice), the way Ares carries Tuesday's Courage and
Wrath at once.

## 2. The two options — mechanism and NAMES

- Setting **`figure_roster`**, one global dropdown in Settings beside
  the Artwork source. Names APPROVED by the owner (2026-07-15):
  **"Pantheon" / "Planetary"** — SR **"Panteon" / "Planetarni"**.
  The seat-by-seat review catalog with all alternatives and open
  dilemmas lives in [The Pantheon Catalog](pantheon_catalog.md).
- Default: **Planetary** (the shipped canon stays untouched).
- Wiring: `WEEKDAY_THEME_FILES_PANTHEON` per theme →
  `assets/weekday/<theme>/pantheon/…`, pantheon articles in
  `symbolism.json`, themes without a pantheon table fall back to
  Planetary (documented). Seats REUSED by both rosters (e.g. Ares,
  Aphrodite) reuse the same art and article — only genuinely new
  figures cost plates and texts.
- The NINTHS are doctrine and hold in BOTH rosters. Duals follow the
  seated sun figure; every changed sun seat needs a pantheon dual
  (flagged per theme below).

### Bee, Elephant, Professions, Religions, Bible, closed sets
- **Bee**: the world-famous triad is Queen/Worker/Drone — our seven
  are named worker CASTES (Nurse, Guard, Builder, Forager), which is
  biologically the famous vocabulary; no swap earns its cost. Noted,
  not proposed.
- **Elephant**: Matriarch/Musth/Allomother are the documentary-famous
  terms; no change.
- **Professions** define the archetypes themselves; **religions/
  bible** already seat by our symbolism (and Bible ships three
  rosters — the proof this mechanism works); **alchemy/japan/zodiac/
  chinese** are closed by nature.
- **Eris & the dwarf planets** (Eris, Haumea, Makemake, Pluto†):
  an Encyclopedia "outer bodies" topic if ever wanted — no weekday,
  no seat; † Pluto would duplicate Hades-the-Ninth.

from the Planetary roster — the topic costs almost nothing for them.
